“Keep Dad in the Game” by joining the Prostate Cancer Foundation and Major League Baseball in the Home Run Challenge!

Participate by pledging a donation for every home run hit from May 20th through Father’s Day on June 18th.

Last year, 621 MLB home runs wound up being hit during the Home Run Challenge! This year, PCF estimates about 458 home runs will be hit. (Even though we hope for more!)

Every donor who makes a gift to this year’s Home Run Challenge will be entered for a chance to win two free tickets to this year’s MLB All-Star Game on July 11th in Seattle, WA!
